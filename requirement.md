# Codeness Studio — Company Profile (Antigravity Requirement)

## Objective
Generate a professional, modern, and brand-consistent **Company Profile** for an IT Consulting company named **Codeness Studio**, ready to be used as content for:
- PDF / proposal document
- Slide deck (PPT) outline
- Website landing page sections

The output must reflect a calm, trustworthy, structured tech-consulting brand.

---

## Brand Identity
### Company Name
**Codeness Studio**

### Tagline
**Start Your Digital Culture with Us**

### Primary Color
- Hex: **#3AAF9F**
- Usage: headings highlights, icons accents, section dividers, call-to-action buttons
- Feel: calm, clean, trustworthy, modern tech

### Logo/Shape Direction
- Circular / rounded shapes preferred
- Smooth, minimal, spacious layout
- Avoid aggressive / overly “salesy” tone and visuals

---

## Target Audience
- UMKM (SMEs)
- Instansi (government / institutions)
- Companies (SMB to enterprise) seeking digital transformation

---

## Company Positioning
Codeness Studio is an **IT Consulting** company focusing on:
1. **Web Application Development**
2. **Microservices Architecture**
3. **UI/UX Design**

Codeness Studio is a **strategic digital partner**, not merely a vendor. Emphasize:
- scalability & maintainability
- clarity of processes
- user-centric design
- measurable outcomes for business operations

---

## Tone & Style Requirements
- Language: **Bahasa Indonesia** (primary)
- Tone: professional, modern, calm, confident, trustworthy
- Avoid: exaggerated claims, hype, “hard selling”, vague marketing buzzwords
- Prefer: clear value statements, structured delivery, concrete approach

---

## Required Deliverables

### A) Company Profile Content (Full Draft)
Produce a full company profile draft with the following structure:

1. **Cover Title**
   - Company name + tagline
   - 1–2 lines positioning statement (short, elegant)

2. **Company Overview**
   - Who we are
   - What we do
   - Who we serve

3. **Vision & Mission**
   - Vision: 1 strong sentence
   - Mission: 3–5 bullet points

4. **Core Services**
   Write each service as:
   - Short definition
   - Key value / impact
   - Example use cases (2–3 bullet points)

   Services:
   - Web Application Development
   - Microservices Architecture
   - UI/UX Design

5. **Our Approach / How We Work**
   Provide a clear 5–7 step process. Example:
   - Discovery & Requirement
   - UX Research / Flow
   - Architecture & Planning
   - Development (Agile)
   - QA & Testing
   - Deployment
   - Monitoring & Improvement

6. **Why Choose Codeness Studio**
   6–8 bullets highlighting strengths:
   - structured delivery
   - product mindset
   - quality & maintainability
   - transparent communication
   - scalable architecture
   - user-centric UI/UX
   - security-aware basics

7. **Target Clients**
   - UMKM
   - Instansi
   - Corporate
   Provide short description per segment

8. **Closing Statement + CTA**
   - professional, inviting
   - include tagline
   - include a placeholder for contact:
     - Email:
     - Phone/WhatsApp:
     - Website:

---

### B) Slide Deck Outline (10–12 Slides)
Create a slide-by-slide outline:
- Slide title
- Key bullets (max 5 bullets per slide)
- Suggested visual/diagram idea

---

### C) One-Page Website Section Copy
Create landing page sections:
- Hero (headline + subheadline + CTA)
- Services (3 cards)
- Process (steps)
- Why Us (bullets)
- CTA section

---

## Design Guidance (Text-only Specs)
Provide layout and style guidance (no images required):
- Preferred: clean white background with subtle gradient accents
- Use #3AAF9F for accents and section headers
- Rounded corners / circular motifs
- Typography suggestion:
  - Heading: Inter / Poppins
  - Body: Inter / Open Sans
- Spacing: generous whitespace, easy scanning

---

## Constraints
- Do NOT include fake portfolio/projects/clients.
- If you need examples, label them clearly as “contoh use case” not as real clients.
- Do NOT output overly long paragraphs; prioritize readability with bullets and sections.
- Keep everything consistent with the brand tone: calm, structured, trustworthy.

---

## Output Format
Return in **Markdown**, with clear headings and sections:
- `## Company Profile Draft`
- `## Slide Deck Outline`
- `## Website Copy`
- `## Design Notes`

End of requirement.
